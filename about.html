My Name is Andrew and I am Web Developer with a passion for Photography, mainly Portrait photography. I am based in Toronto, Ontario.

Why Photography?

My interest in photography was first kindled in 2014 upon meeting my partner. Upon examining various Instagram profiles, I was struck by the captivating imagery of individuals and their companions in scenic locations worldwide. This prompted me to ponder whether I possessed the ability to achieve similar outcomes. My aim was to enhance my partner's self-assurance and capture her beauty.

Over the course of several years, I experimented with various camera models but found that the resulting images faileld to meet what I envisioned. It was not until 2017 that I acquired my initial full-frame mirrorless camera, accompanied by a 50mm lens. This marked a turning point, as it enabled me to achieve a creative vision I had previously encountered in the Instagram profiles.

In 2021, I resolved to create a website with the aim of showcasing my artistic vision, personal journey, and the progress I have achieved thus far. My goal is to expand my skills beyond portrait photography and explore landscape/car photography. While I am still an amateur, I am committed to improvement and growth, with this website serving as a visual documentation of my artistic journey.
